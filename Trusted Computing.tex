\begin{abstract}
This report discusses trusted computing concepts in the context of
Internet of Things (IoT) devices. We examine the goals, core techniques,
and inherent limitations of applying hardware-based security mechanisms
such as TPMs, Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs), and remote
attestation to resource-constrained IoT deployments.
\end{abstract}
